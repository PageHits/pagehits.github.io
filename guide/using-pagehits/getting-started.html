Getting Started

What is PageHits?

PageHits is a managed platform for you to design and publish landing pages. So you can generate leads for your business. Promote your ideas. Or run campaigns. Validate hypotheses. Test product-market fit. The possibilities are endless.

Unlike casual website builders, PageHits has been built with your business objectives in mind. We know how crucial experimentation is for taking key decisions that shape the journey of a company. Which is why PageHits provides a robust yet easy AB-testing capability.

With PageHits, you should be able to launch your landing pages in a matter of minutes - without any prior knowledge in coding or design.

Who should use PageHits?

Anyone who needs to conduct market outreach should benefit from PageHits.

Whether you're validating a product idea, evaluating product-market fit, promoting your services, launching new products, running ad campaigns PageHits's landing pages makes it easy to reach your goals quicker.

Prerequisites

You need zero experience with coding, design. Or even any prior knowledge of server infrastructure, since PageHits is a managed platform.

However, to successfully achieve your business goals, you should have a strong conceptual understanding of marketing concepts in general. That's where this wiki comes in - full of howtos, guides and general articles that helps you stay up to date and on the top of your game.

Project organization

Spaces

Think of a space as a box for your project - where you keep everything belonging to the project all together. And separate from other projects that you might be collaborating on with other teams.

So if you're working on only a single project, it's simple. You're within your default space.

If however, you're dealing with multiple projects - such as a consultant with multiple clients or two separate teams within the same company working on two different campaigns, you would need to use spaces to manage project contexts.

Creating a space

When you sign up for your account, a space is automatically created for you by default.

Switching space

Users

There can be 4 types of users in your space.

  • Owner The creator of the space. Has all admin privileges, and is the only one on the team to request deleting the space There can be only one single owner for a project and transfering ownership can be done only by contacting support.
  • Admin Can do pretty much anything, except deleting the space. Can manage the team - includign inviting, edit and delete other member Change plans. Update payment methods.
  • Editor Can create, build and delete pages and page-variants
  • Viewer Read only access Can preview pages that have been created by other team-members Can view non-sensitive settings.

Users other than viewers count towards the number of users on your plan.
